Assembling a Single Pressure Sensor

Step 1 - Cut Velostat

Using the provided pattern cut a circle of velostat from the sheet using scissors

 

Step 2 - Gather cardboard and cut copper tape

Take the 2 smaller, round cardboard pieces from the provided packet

Cut 2 pieces of cardboard tape. They should be 1cm longer than the circle+tab

Step 3 - Apply Tape

Apply 1 piece of copper tape to each piece of cardboard so that it goes from the edge of the circle to the end of the tab. Wrap the excess length around the tab to the underneath.

 

Step 4 - Place Velostat on top of 1 cardboard piece

Be sure that the long piece of copper tape is in contact with the Velostat

Step 5 - Place Top cardboard piece on top of Velostat

Copper Tape should be touching the velostat

Tab should be oriented in the opposite direction as the bottom cardboard layer

Step 6 - Secure the sensor sandwich with tape

Note how the copper tape wraps to the bottom of the tab
