
Overview
A key method for developing projects is to connect your own work with examples across a variety of media. For this workshop, you will create a first version.

Activity
- Establish a first simple description of your project.
- - 3/4 sentences 

- Find existing work, media, and writings that connect to key aspects of your project.
- The types of work you must Find:
- - Project on a social media platform
- - - 1-2 sentence description of its connection to your project
- - - An image of the Project
- - - Link to the post
- - Project on a website
- - - 1-2 sentence description of its connection to your project
- - - An image of the Project
- - - Link to the project
- - Conference paper
- - - 3/4 sentences summarizing the paper and its relationship to your project
- - Physical Book from OCADU Library
- - - Image of the Book
- - - 3/4 sentences summarizing the paper and its relationship to your project


Deliverable
Make a post here that includes the above information

Students taking 0.5 credit Thesis:
1 Social Media post
1 website
1 Conference paper
1 Book

Students taking 1.0 credit Thesis:
2 Social Media post
2 website
2 Conference paper
2 Book

Due
To be completed in class today, but you have until next week if needed.
