Playtest 1 is an opportunity to get direct feedback on an early version of your project. It should focus on specific aspects of the work and defined by clear questions.

Deliverables
  • 0.5 credit - 1 session / 1.0 credit - 2 sessions
Main Image
  • This sketch/image/render/screenshot should be the best visual you have to show what was tested
General Overview
  • 1 - 3 sentences describing what you are testing
Questions
  • The questions asked during the test and when they occurred. Before, during, after
Findings
  • 3 - 4 bullets outlining issues learned from the session
