This presentation marks the first major checkpoint for your thesis project. It is your opportunity to share where your project stands, articulate the ideas driving the work, and outline how you plan to move forward toward V2.

Deliverables
  • 0.5 credit
Overview

Give a presentation that explains the current state of your project. It should provide a general overview of the project, progress so far, and the plan for the next stage.

  • What
    • 2-3 sentences describing your project in its current form
    • Images and Videos that describe what it is
  • Why
    • Identify 1-2 broader themes, research areas, or fields you are exploring with the work
  • How
    • Bullet-point plan with approximate dates for key steps between now and V2
Main Image
  • Include one hero image, screenshot, render, or sketch that best represents the current state of your project.
Presentation Format
  • 10 minutes presentation, 5 minutes feedback (15 minutes total per project)
  • You can demo the state of things, but pre-recorded video is encouraged where possible for the sake of time
  • Slides are expected (Powerpoint, Google Slides, Canva, etc.)
Session Logistics

We will follow a similar layout to the playtest of setting up 4 projects on the 4 TVs and then going through them 1 by 1.

Feedback

Though this isn't a Playtest, you are expected to give feedback to your peers. You did a good job of this for the Playtest, so please continue that practice here.

Submission
  • Submit your presentation as a URL (if using Powerpoint, Google Slides, Canva, etc.) or as a PDF.
